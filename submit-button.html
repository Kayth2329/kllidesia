OFW Requirements

(Any 2 Government-Issued Valid IDs)

    Primary IDs
  • Philippine Passport
  • Driver's License
  • UMID
  • PRC ID
  • SSS ID (with birthdate)
  • Postal ID
  • National ID
    • Secondary IDs
  • TIN
  • Philhealth
  • Voter's ID
  • Barangay ID
  • SSS ID (without birthdate)
  • HDMF/Loyalty (Required, if HDMF financing)
  • PWD ID (w/ birthdate, address, photo, signature)

*Photocopy of the required IDs need to have 3 specimen signatures next to it.
**Submitted IDs must be 6 months before expiration.
***Additional ID: Company ID

Proof of Income (All of the Following):

  • Latest Job Contract
  • COEC
  • 3 months Pay slip

Proof of Billing (At Least 1)

  • Electric Bill
  • Water Bill
  • Cable
  • Credit Card
  • Postpaid/Landline

Must Be Submitted Within 8 to 30 Days:

  • Special Power of Attorney (Notarized or Consularized)
  • Three (3) pcs. 1x1 ID Picture
  • MRI ABOVE NEL FORM - For above 5M TCP
  • MANULIFE Application
  • Notarized Certificate of Employment and Compensation
  • Notarized or Authenticated: SPA on BIS, RA8 & CTC/SPA
  • Signed/Printed Contract To Sell
  • Filled-up and Signed Bank/HDMF Application Form
  • Company ID
  • Birth Certificate
  • Latest One Month Pay slip
  • Legal Separation Papers (if applicable)
  • Death Certificate (if applicable)
  • Marriage Contract (if applicable)
  • Personal Interview
  • Latest ITR 2316 (EMPLOYED)
  • CENOMAR
  • MID OR PAG-IBIG ID
  • TIN ID AND TIN NUMBER VERIFICATION
  • ESSA/LATEST PAG-IBIG RECEIPT
  • 2 Latest Valid IDs of Spouse
